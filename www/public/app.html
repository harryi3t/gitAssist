Welcome to SlackAssist

SlackAsist is slack bot that can help you with some very common tasks that require you to check the browser. It can help you with your PR (pull request) status, what your team's member been doing and much more.

The bot is still under development. But you can contribute on this project here

To access the private repositories of your github account, we need an access token. Please click on the button to authenticate our app to receive the token.
